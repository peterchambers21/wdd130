Soups

Soups are a stample in the Chambers family, especially during the winter. Nothing is better than a nice warm bowl of food after a long day playing in the snow. From hearty potato to savory pumpkin, we have a soup for everyones tastes and needs.

Sweet Spot Sweet Potato Soup by Becky Cornwall

1 Tbsp butter
1 onion, chopped
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp coriander
pinch of cayenne
1 tsp salt
2 lb sweet potatoes
4 cups chicken broth
2 Tbsp chopped parsley or cilantro

Heat butter in 4-quart soup pot. Add onion and saute until soft. Add spices and salt. Peel sweet potatoes and cut into chunks. Add sweet potatoes and broth to onions. Bring to boil. Lower heat, cover and simmer for about 30 minutes, until potatoes are fork tender. Blend soup in small batches in electric blender until smooth. Garnish with parsley or cilantro.

Cheesy Broccoli Soup by Becky Cornwall

2 onions, chopped
3 cups broccoli, chopped
3 cloves garlic, crushed
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 quart chicken broth, canned or homemade
1 cup low-fat cheddar cheese, shredded 1 cup skim milk

In a large soup pot, saute onions and garlic until wilted. Add chopped broccoli and continue cooking. Pour broth over the top and cook until broccoli is very tender, about 30 minutes. In batches, process soup through a food processor or blender until smooth. Add milk and let continue cooking another 20 minutes, but be careful not to let boil or the soup will separate. When nice and thick, remove from heat and serve in individual bowls. Top each bowl with 1⁄4 cup cheese and serve with a big salad and whole-grain bread.
